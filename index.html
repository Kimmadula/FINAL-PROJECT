Camote Island

The Camotes Islands are located in the Camotes Sea, off the coast of the Province of Cebu.
It is known for its crystal clear waters, extremely fine white sand, and the spectacular
view of sunset that it offers.

Moalboal

Kawasan Falls is the most popular waterfall in Cebu and for good reason!
There are several lovely waterfalls, perfect for swimming.
Plus there are plenty of restaurants and adventure opportunities.
It gets crowded so arrive early for a more enjoyable experience.

Bantayan Island

Bantayan Island is an off-the-beaten-path destination in Cebu that is said to be the Boracay of the south!
There are so many awesome things to do in Bantayan Island including white sand beaches, saltwater
lagoons, mangrove boardwalks, cave pools, old ruins, skydiving, and island hopping, to name a few.
